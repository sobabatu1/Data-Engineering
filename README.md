# 🏰 Fortress SaaS: Data Engineering & Financial Health Pipeline

## The Mission
Fortress is a high-growth SaaS platform. As the business scaled to 10,000+ users, the leadership team needed a "Fortress-grade" data infrastructure to protect recurring revenue and identify churn risk in real-time.
